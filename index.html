WHO WE ARE

Social Mood Label is a digital-first platform that documents, questions, and reflects on how societal conditions shape collective mood. We explore themes such as; Recreation for well being, Children and youth advocacy, Masculinity, Violence against women, Fashion and identity.

#Trending

Current Campaign

Our latest campaign, 'Matcha, Identity & the Absence of Youth Spaces' explores how the rise of matcha has evolved beyond a drink into a powerful social symbol, one amplified by influencers and social media trends.

We reflect on how this culture pressures adolescents to consume and conform in order to connect, even when access and affordability are out of reach.
